HOW TO PREVENT?

Wear A Mask

Wear a mask if they must be around other people.

Wash your Hand

Wash your hand regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Consult

Seek medical care early if you or your child has a fever cough or difficulty breathing.

Avoid Touching

Avoid crowds ans poorly ventilated space,

Avoid contact

Avoid close contact with anyone who has cold or flu-like symptom.

Clean everyday

Clean and disinfect frequently touched surface.
